Nice to meet you

I am an aspiring front-end developer based in A Coruña, Spain. For the past six years, I have worked as a customer support officer for a small tech company that operates entirely remotely. This experience has allowed me to gain insights into technology, software testing, and customer experience.

Working in a remote environment not only enabled me to relocate permanently to Spain but also provided me with the opportunity to acquire new skills and explore potential career paths.

My interest in web design and development has always been present; I even created a simple website using only HTML during middle school. However, I never viewed it as a viable career option until now.

Collaborating closely with developers, product managers, and data engineers reignited my curiosity about the world of coding. This inspired me to take the leap and pursue a career as a front-end designer and developer!
